# Standard comparison dimensions

- **Positioning** — the one-line pitch each competitor leads with.
- **Target segment** — company size, vertical, buyer persona.
- **Pricing model** — per-seat, usage, flat; published or sales-gated.
- **Core differentiator** — the thing customers cite in reviews.
- **Weaknesses** — recurring complaints from G2/Reddit/HN, churn drivers.
- **Momentum** — funding, hiring, release cadence over the last year.
- **Ecosystem** — integrations, marketplace, community size.

Pick 4-6 dimensions relevant to the decision; do not use all of them by default.
